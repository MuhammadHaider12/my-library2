Library Terms and Conditions
Membership Agreement

By registering as a student member, you agree to:

  • Use library resources responsibly and ethically
  • Return borrowed materials by the due date
  • Pay any applicable fines for overdue materials
  • Not damage or deface library property
  • Follow all library rules and regulations
  • Keep your account information secure
Privacy Policy

Your personal information will be used solely for library management purposes and will not be shared with third parties without your consent.
